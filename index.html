At Frank Karanja & Co. Advocates, we are a trusted law firm based in Nairobi, Kenya, offering expert legal services in Civil, Family, Business, Education, Criminal, and Cyber Law.

Our mission is to provide reliable legal representation with integrity, efficiency, and excellence — helping clients find justice, clarity, and peace of mind.

Civil Law

We handle disputes between individuals and organizations, ensuring fair settlements and justice through strong legal representation.

Family Law

Compassionate support in divorce, custody, and marital disputes - protecting your family’s rights and future.

Business Law

Education Law
